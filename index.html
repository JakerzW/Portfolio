Biography

I live at the intersection of design, art and technology.

Over the past decade I’ve worked in all areas of game development. From designing and programming mechanics to brainstorming new features, creating or collaborating on art and meticulously testing builds, I’ve done it all – and loved every second of it.

As a dedicated gamer across almost every genre, it’s become apparent to me that sadly not all games prioritise the player first. I strongly believe that only with the player as the primary focus can you make a brilliant game. I make sure to bring that player-first mindset to every game I work on.

My time as a player has sharpened my design instincts, directly informing my efforts to make unique and exciting games. The blend of my programming background and creative skillset allow me to bridge engineering and art fluently, ensuring efficient development and smooth collaboration.

My creative brain doesn’t stop at games, either. Whether it’s composing atmospheric soundtracks or music, capturing photographs, crafting worlds in D&D or creating digital art, I treat every hobby as a chance to engage with new perspectives and stretch my design legs.

What I Bring to a Team

  • Creative and technical balance: A multi-disciplinary skill set that allows me to script mechanics and prototype concepts in-engine myself, while collaborating closely with art and development teams to design custom tools that make their workflows easier.
  • Attitude and collaboration: An eager creator and problem solver who loves diving into problems alongside others. I value transparency, direct feedback and keeping the focus entirely on what is best for the project. For me, human happiness and great chemistry always come first, because an inclusive, collaborative environment is what actually breeds incredible games.
  • Proven systems and scale: Hands-on experience designing core gameplay loops, systemic mechanics and curated level experiences. I focus on building integrated, scalable systems that connect dynamically for a cohesive, immersive game, proven to run smoothly for hundreds of millions of players.
  • Platform and genre agnostic: A design philosophy built on fundamental player psychology, making me entirely adaptable to any project. I am just as comfortable tuning systems for mobile as I am designing deep PC and console mechanics or shaping creative, artistic visions.
